What is Krav Maga?

Krav Maga means “hand to hand combat” in Hebrew. It was a military system developed by Imi Lichtenfeld in the late 1940’s and was taught to the Israeli Defense Force.

Krav Maga emphasised threat neutralisation, simultaneous defensive, offensive maneouvers and aggression applications.

Krav Maga has since been adapted for both civilian and military application.
