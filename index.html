Who are we?

Crave is an exciting new food & coffee brand providing the best ‘on the go’ food & drinks in South Africa.

We're all about providing fresh and healthy food, ready and waiting for the busy person on the go.

Our mix of retail convenience with a café vibe has been a huge hit with all of our loyal customers, so we are now planning to roll out this success to other busy areas of South Africa by offering franchise opportunities. Contact us if you want ot be a part of it!

Our Food

Although we serve exceptionally good coffee, and quite a lot of it, we are best known for our fabulous food.

Our food is fresh, and prepared in our own kitchens. Aside from this, at the end of the day if it’s not sold it goes to charity, and we start all over again the following morning. That’s why there are no sell-by dates on our sandwiches.

We are always looking for lovely new suppliers of unusual, homemade treats. We like to think of ourselves as a bit of an ‘urban farmstore’, so please contact us if you have any great new products or ideas.

Healthy

‘Healthy fresh food fast’ is what we are all about: from sandwiches to smoothies. The menu is full of lovely, tasty stuff made the way you would make it at home.

Fresh

All of our goods are freshly prepared in our on-site kitchens each morning, packaged up, and placed out front.
We use fresh ingredients and start from scratch each and every day.

Fast

We cater for those on the go, boasting speedy in-house food, as well as an extensive ‘platter menu’ for any of your breakfast, lunchtime or early evening functions, ready to go.
